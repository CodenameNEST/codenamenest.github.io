Benefits of joining our team
- You do what you want - sometimes there are requests, but remember that you can show us your own ideas!
- There is no deadline - the only thing that matters is are you going to make something and finish it, not how long it will take.
- We don't make games for money, just for fun. If you want to earn money though - you can contact knuxfanwin8 (the team's owner)
Codename: NEST is like a "freestyle" studio - you make (original) art you think of, if you think it's good you show it on the server, and maybe we'll use it in a game. Sometimes there will be requests for certain things for you to make. You don't have to strictly just make the thing you were hired for, though - if you have an interesting concept of anything, you can still show it to us.
